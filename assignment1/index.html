About Me

Los Angeles is full of people who make their living in all sorts of professions, but are wannabe actors. Eric Zuckerman is a professional actor who is a wannabe coder.


You may have seen him on The Office, or in War of the Worlds. Or maybe you saw him as the rabbi in season three of Orange is the New Black. He's particularly proud of that performance, but hopefully the next time he works for Netflix, it will be implementing code to make their database better at predicting what he wants to watch.
